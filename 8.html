About the Pulmonologist Department

Our Pulmonologist Department specializes in the treatment of various lung and respiratory disorders. We offer expert care for conditions such as asthma, COPD, pneumonia, tuberculosis, and lung cancer. Our team of skilled pulmonologists is equipped with state-of-the-art diagnostic tools and treatment methods.

Services include:

  • Pulmonary Function Testing (PFT)
  • Bronchoscopy and Thoracoscopy
  • Management of Asthma and Allergies
  • Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD) Management
  • Sleep Apnea Treatment
  • Respiratory Rehabilitation

Our team is committed to providing the highest level of care and ensuring the best outcomes for our patients suffering from respiratory conditions.
