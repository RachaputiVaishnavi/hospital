About the Orthopedics Department

Our Orthopedics Department specializes in the treatment of musculoskeletal disorders. With a team of experienced surgeons and advanced facilities, we provide comprehensive care for fractures, joint replacements, sports injuries, and spine-related conditions.

Services include:

  • Arthroscopic Surgeries
  • Joint Replacement (Knee, Hip, Shoulder)
  • Fracture Management
  • Physical Therapy and Rehabilitation
  • Spinal Surgeries

Our team is committed to providing state-of-the-art care to ensure the best outcomes for our patients.
