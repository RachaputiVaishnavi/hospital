About the General Surgery Department

Our General Surgery Department is dedicated to providing high-quality surgical care for various medical conditions. We offer advanced treatment options and minimally invasive techniques to ensure the best outcomes for our patients.

Services include:

  • Appendectomy (Removal of Appendix)
  • Cholecystectomy (Gallbladder Removal)
  • Hernia Repair
  • Colorectal Surgery
  • Laparoscopic Surgery
  • Breast Cancer Surgery
  • Thyroid Surgery

Our skilled surgeons are committed to providing safe and effective care, ensuring a smooth recovery for each patient.
